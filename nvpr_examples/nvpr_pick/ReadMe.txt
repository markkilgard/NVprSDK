
nvpr_pick

An NV_path_rendering example demonstrating how to query in object (path)
space whether a point is inside or outside the fill and/or stroke of a
path object.

Click on the image to test a point.

Use '+' and '-' to advance/rewind through the path test cases.

Press 'n' to test a "bed of nails" set of points.

Rotate and zooming is centered where you first left mouse click.
Hold down Ctrl at left mouse click to JUST SCALE.
Hold down Shift at left mouse click to JUST ROTATE.

Use middle mouse button to slide (translate).

Space bar toggles warping animation.
