
nvpr_tiger3d

An NV_path_rendering example showing four PostScript tiger instances
drawn in a perspective 3D view surounding a wireframe (or filled) teapot.
Overlaid text says "Path rendering and 3D meet!"

This example uses no texturing, multi-pass, or off-screen buffers.
All the rendering included the path-rendered tigers is drawn in a single
pass.

Left click & drag will spin the 3D scene.

'+' and '-' increase/decrease the teapot size.

't' toggles the teapot between wireframe and filled

's' toggles stroking of tigers

'f' toggles filling of tigers
