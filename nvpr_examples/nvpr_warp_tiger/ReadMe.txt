
nvpr_warp_tiger

An NV_path_rendering example showing how projective transformation
during rendering are easy to accomplish.

You can left click & drag the purple balls at each corner of the tiger's
bounding box to change the tiger's projection.

Space bar toggles animating the purple control points.

'f' toggles filling.

's' toggles stroking.

'F' shows frames/second counter.

'r' resets the scene.

Rotate and zooming is centered where you first left mouse click.
Hold down Ctrl at left mouse click to JUST SCALE.
Hold down Shift at left mouse click to JUST ROTATE.

Use middle mouse button to slide (translate).
